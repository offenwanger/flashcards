About this Application

How it works

To use the flashcards program, open the menu in the top right corner () and select your languages. The available langues are the synthesized voices available in your browser. The system will use the synthesized voices to read your flashcards. Leave these options blank if you wish the application to remain silent.

Next, add some flashcards using the add flashcards option in the menu ( Add Flashcards).

To use the application, press the play button (). This will randomly choose a flashcard, and read out the prompt to you (it randomly selects which language to read). Press play again to see the text for what was read. Click play one final time to see the text for the response, which will also be read.

Important!

All of your flashcards and scores are stored on your device in cookies. If you clear your cookies, all your data will be lost. Please make sure to download your flashcards ( Download Flashcards) and store the text file somewhere safe before you clear your cookies. Then you can upload it ( Upload Flashcards) when you're done, and continue from where you left off.
